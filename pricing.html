Below you can find all our offers with additional information of the service that will be provided and what you and your pets can expect, when you leave them in our care.

You have an important meeting or need to leave the city for a day and have nowhere to leave your best friend!?

We are here for you! Bring your friend and we will keep him occupied!

You can leave your dog with us for up to 8 hrs. We are open from 8am till 7pm (drop-offs before 8am are also possible, but need to be arranged before hand. Please check the Contacts page to get in touch with us.)

We will be happy to keep your dog with us over night! He will have his own room with all the necessities. You can choose to either bring food from home or for additional price, we will arrange splendid meals from our long time dog-food provider.

Your dog will be able to play with other guests in our backyard and in addition will have minimum 2 long walks through the nearby forest.

We know that current life is demanding and due to work it's not always possible to take your friend for a longer walk. But Hey! we can also help you out with that! We will be happy to arrange times to take your dog for a proper walk. This option however is only available to Members so be sure to register and fill out the formulare and we will see how best to arrange that! All our employees are certified dog-coaches, so you don't, your dog will be in good hands!

Additionally we offer services such as:

  • Taking your dog to a vet appointment
  • Pick-up and return to our hotel if you are not able to bring him yourself

We know that not everyone is as dog friendly as we and it can happen that you planned to celebrate the New Year, but can't take your dog with you. We are happy to help and take care of your friend while you celebrate the new beginnings! Check out our New Year's offer. It's a package deal, where we will provide a safe place for your friend!
